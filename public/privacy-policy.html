Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 8, 2025 — This policy explains how HeartLink Dating ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares your information.

1. Introduction

HeartLink Dating is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to our mobile app, website, and related services (collectively, the "Service"). By using the Service you agree to the collection and use of information described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, information from your use of the Service, and information from third parties.

  • Account & Profile Data: name, username, email, phone number (if provided), profile photos, bio, gender, preferences, and other profile fields.
  • Messages & Content: messages, likes, matches, and content you share with other users. Note: while messages between users may be encrypted, we may temporarily store message metadata to deliver and moderate the Service.
  • Payment Info: billing information and receipts for purchases (payments are processed by third‑party providers — we do not store full credit card numbers).
  • Device & Usage Data: device type, operating system, app version, IP address, language, timestamps, features used, and crash reports.
  • Location Data: approximate location derived from IP and optional precise location if you enable location services to use location-based features.
  • Third‑Party Data: profile information or social graph if you sign in with third‑party services (e.g., Apple, Google, Facebook) and permit us to access that data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service, including matching, messaging, recommendations, and personalized content.
  • To process transactions and send receipts or communications related to purchases.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, abuse, fraud, and other harmful activity.
  • To send administrative messages, security alerts, and support responses.
  • With your consent, to send marketing and promotional communications (you can opt out at any time).

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Other Users: profile information, photos, and public fields when you interact (e.g., likes, matches, messages) are visible to other users as part of the Service.
  • Service Providers: vendors who perform services on our behalf (payments, analytics, hosting, push notifications). They only receive the data necessary to perform their service.
  • Safety & Legal: to comply with legal obligations, respond to lawful requests, protect rights, safety, property, or prevent fraud or illegal activity.
  • Business Transfers: in connection with a merger, sale, reorganization, or acquisition — users will be notified and data will remain subject to this policy or the purchaser's policies.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We and our partners use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to operate the Service, analyze usage, and deliver targeted content. You can control cookies through your browser or device settings, but disabling some cookies may affect functionality.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as necessary to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You can request deletion of your account and data — see Section 9.

7. Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your data. However no system is completely secure — you should take care when sharing personal information online.

8. Children

The Service is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16; if we learn we have collected such data we will take steps to delete it.

9. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & Portability: You may request a copy of certain personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct your profile information at any time via account settings.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account from the app settings or by contacting us; deletion may not remove content shared with other users (e.g., messages they saved).
  • Marketing Opt‑Out: Opt out of promotional emails by using the unsubscribe link in those emails or via account settings.

To exercise these rights, contact us (see Section 12) and we will respond within applicable legal timeframes.

10. International Transfers

We operate globally and may transfer and store your information in servers located in other countries. Where required, we apply appropriate safeguards to protect your information.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. If changes are material, we will provide notice via the app, email, or prominent notice on our site, and update the "Last updated" date above.
